Working Agreement v1.3 — Key Rules
Jira Workflow · Status Definitions (from WA v1.3)
🟦To Do — Refined stories with DoR met, or Epic approved for intake/discovery but not yet started.
🟡In Progress — Active development, configuration, or discovery work underway.
🟠In Review — Awaiting PM review OR Epic is in Hypercare (development complete; stabilization in progress).
🟢Done — Completed/moved to PRD, OR Epic completed Hypercare and transitioned to Steady State.
🔴Blocked / External Dependency Hold — Unable to proceed due to outside teams, vendor, access, or business decisions. Block reason + next checkpoint required in Jira comments.
Definition of Done (DoD) · Definition of Ready (DoR)
✅Done when: All AC completed and validated · peer review done · tested in DEV/QA/SIT/UAT as applicable · PM approval received · documentation updated · Epics only: Hypercare complete and bot transitioned to Steady State.
📋Ready when: User story in As a / I want / So that format · AC clearly defined, verifiable, testable · linked to Epic · reviewed by team at Refinement or Sprint Planning · dependencies and assumptions identified and documented.
Pulling Work & Sprint Rules
→Decisions to pull new work are discussed during 16th-Minute and confirmed via team consensus at DSU.
→Once sprint started, no new stories added without 16th-Minute discussion or prior SM approval.
→Discovery/exploratory work may occur without sprint stories, but must remain visible at Epic level.
→Stories >8 pts must be split. Bugs are not pointed. Sprint Reviews: only when business-validated, production-ready work exists to demo.
